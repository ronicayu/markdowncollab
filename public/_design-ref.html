Q3 project brief

M Morgan Chen updated 4 minutes ago draft

The team is shipping a focused set of improvements this quarter. This brief captures the what and why; the spec doc covers the how.

Goals

  • ✓Align on metric definitions with data team
  • Reduce churn by 15% by end of Q3
  • Ship the onboarding revamp by Aug 15
  • Run 12 qualitative interviews before lock

Non-goals

We are explicitly not rebuilding the editor surface this quarter. See roadmap-q4.md for the broader surface rework.

Success metrics

  1. Week-4 retention ≥ 42% (baseline 36%)
  2. First-run completion ≥ 78% (baseline 61%)
  3. Agent-assisted sessions ≥ 3 per active user per week
"Agents shouldn't feel like a feature bolted on — they should feel like another teammate in the doc." — team principle, Mar kickoff

Risks & unknowns

Data pipeline for week-4 retention isn't in place yet; we're tracking a proxy from event logs. Research agent has flagged that the baseline may be stale (source: user panel Q1).

Timeline

  • Wk 1–2 — scope lock, spec review
  • Wk 3–6 — build, with weekly check-ins
  • Wk 7 — internal dogfood
  • Wk 8–9 — staged rollout, 5% → 50% → 100%
